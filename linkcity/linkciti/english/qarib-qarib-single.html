Irrfan Khan Is Simply Superb In This Rom Com “Qarib Qarib Singlle” Trailer

October 31, 2017

Qarib Qarib Singlle Trailer looks like a fun-filled comedy ride that is going to be loved by the audience. Irrfan Khan is acing the comic timing and Parvathy matches him at every portion. The film is shot in various cities on the banks of the river Ganga. The two characters start their journey as Qarib Qarib Singlle and will probably find love on their way.

 

Tanuja Chandra has returned to the director’s chair with this movie after a long gap of 11 years. It will be a new change to watch a slice of life film from the director.
